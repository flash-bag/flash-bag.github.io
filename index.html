Flash Bag More than a bag…

Flash Bag obtains its energy from the sun.Defines the objects placed in it with the help of the square code and charges the electronic devices non-contact.

MORE DETAILS

Project Features

The book we put in is a bag that documents you what you have forgotten by reading the square code or barcode we placed on objects such as a document laptop or comparing it with your tomorrow's lesson or work schedule. In addition, thanks to the solar systems on it, it generates electricity from solar energy and it will use it to charge my phone or laptop wirelessly inside the bag. In addition, there will be a heating system on the back of the bag and your back will be warm in winter. Thanks to this bag we have designed by combining all these features; We do not forget the materials we need to bring with us for tomorrow, we can charge our phone or laptop without having to search for a socket or powerbank in the outside, and we are less affected by cold in the winter months.

Solar panel features Use of Solar Panel

Solar energy is an energy that is widely used today, clean, easy to store and everywhere you can reach to the sunlight. Thanks to the panels on the bag, solar energy is stored in the battery and energy is supplied to the electronic devices that need energy in the bag.These devices are electric charger, heater and controller. The battery converts the stored solar energy into electrical energy and distributes them proportionally to the instantaneous energy requirement of the energy devices. As a result, the electronic devices are wirelessly charged with the electric energy obtained from the solar energy and ın order to use in cold weather, the electric energy is converted into heat energy and using this electric energy in square code reader is most important feature.

Square Code ReaderController Operation

A system based on the relationship between the sensors in the rucksack and the data matrix on our items. Data matrix that we can temporarily place on our items and can be repeatedly inserted and removed without harming our items are being detected and identified via sensors where been on our rucksack. Obtained data is being sent to the mobile application via the wireless. It is comparing about presence situation of objects that be expressed by datas which come from sensors and the plan already given to the application. According to this comparison the user is being notified.

Solved Problems

Due to having different programs according to the needs of daily or business life, the problem of identifying the materials that people should keep with them and the problem of forgetting the materials to be placed in the bag. As a solution to these problems, it is necessary to inform the software system of the bag according to the work schedule and to remind the users when the time of these materials is available. In addition, as a solution to the energy problem, there is a solar panel on the outer surface of the bag and the energy of this solar panel's wireless charger, heater and square code reader system. A wireless charging mechanism that takes energy from a solar panel as a solution to the problem of charging in places where electronic devices do not have an electrical source.
